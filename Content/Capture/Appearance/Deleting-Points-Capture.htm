Deleting Points in Objects

When you edit lines or polygons, you can delete points (the small colored circles in the object) in order to turn two line segments into one, thus changing the shape of the object.

 how to delete points in objects
  1. Open an image containing an object, such as a line or polygon.
  2. Click on the object.

    • Click on the point in the object that you want to delete (i.e., one of the small colored circles). Then in the local toolbar click .

      OR

    • Right-click on the point in the object that you want to delete (i.e., one of the small colored circles) and from the context menu select Delete Point.
