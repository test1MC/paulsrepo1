How to Move Tasks to Milestones by Dragging

  3. Locate the task card, then click and drag it to one of the other milestone columns (To Do, In Progress, Completed).

    You cannot drag tasks to the Archive or Backlog area. You can only drag them to the milestone columns.

 How to Move Tasks to Change the Order

  3. Locate the task card, then click and drag it up or down in its milestone column.

 How to Move Tasks Using Menu Options
  3. Locate the task card and right-click it.

  4. In the context menu, select Move. Then choose the location where you want to move the task.

You also open a task, click Move in the upper-right corner, and use the context menu.

 How to Move Tasks Out of Backlog or Archive
  2. At the top of the page, select Backlog or Archive.
  3. In the grid, click the name of the task.
  4. In the upper-right corner of the dialog, select Move.
  5. In the context menu, select the location where you want to move the task.
  6. Click Save.
