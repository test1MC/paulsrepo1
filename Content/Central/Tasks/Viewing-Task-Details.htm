Viewing Task Details

How to View Task Details
  2. Select any of the views at the top (Board, Grid, Calendar, Backlog, Archive).

  3. Click the name of the task.

  4. (Optional) From this dialog, you can do the following, depending on your permission settings:
    • Edit You can make various changes to the task after clicking in the different fields. See Editing Tasks.
    • Move At the top-right of the dialog, you can click Move in order to place the task in a different milestone (e.g., To Do, In Progress, Completed). See Moving Tasks.
    • Delete At the top-right of the dialog, you can click Delete in order to remove the task. See Deleting Tasks.
    • Add Comments You can use the Discussion area to add comments related to the task. You can also click the paper clip icon to add attachments related to your comment. See Adding Comments to Tasks.
    • Add Attachments You can use the Attachments area to choose one or more files to be associated with the task. Others can then download the attached files for more information about the task.
  5. When finished, click Save.
