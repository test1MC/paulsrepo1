 Notes

If you want to clean out files in order to save on storage space, you can delete files that are attached to individual tasks.

To do this, click the ellipsis at the end of the attachment, then select Delete.

In addition to editing the details for a task, you can change its state by moving it from one milestone to another (e.g., from “To Do” to “In Progress”). To do this, you can open a task and click Move in the upper-right corner of the dialog. Alternatively, you can right-click the task card in the Board view and make a selection from the context menu. See Moving Tasks.
