Purchasing User Seats and Storage Space

If you are working in trial mode, this process will let you buy a Central subscription. Once the purchase is made, you will no longer be in trial mode, but rather full mode.

How to Purchase User Seats or Storage Space

  1. Settings Dialog

    1. At the top of Central, click your user name, then click Settings.
    2. At the top of the dialog, click the License tab.

    Storage and Usage Widget

    1. If you’ve added the Storage and Usage widget (see About Widgets), click Home on the left side of the interface to open the Home page.
    2. In the widget, click Purchase.
  2. On the left side of the dialog, click Billing, and complete the fields to add a credit card. Then click Save, and in the confirmation dialog click Proceed.
  3. Select the General tab.

  4. Depending on whether you want to add Storage, Authors, and/or Subject Matter Experts, click in the appropriate Add field and enter a number. Storage space is sold in increments of 10 GB, with the first 10 being free. As you enter numbers, Central calculates the total cost and displays at the bottom of the dialog (scroll down in the dialog to see it).

    Notes
  5. Click Continue.
  6. Review your purchase details and click Purchase, then in the dialog click OK.
 Note

If you need to cancel your subscription, you can simply remove your credit card information from the system.
