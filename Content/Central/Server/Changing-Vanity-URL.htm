How to Change the Prefix (Subdomain) for the License Vanity URL
  1. At the top of Central, click your user name, then click Settings.
  2. In the dialog, select the License tab.
  3. On the left, click URL.
  4. In the Vanity name field, enter the prefix you want to use for your vanity URL (only alpha characters and numbers are allowed).
  5. Click Save.
