Creating Teams

How to Create a Team
  1. On the left side of the interface, click Teams.
  2. In the toolbar click .
  3. On the Settings tab, complete the Name field.
  4. (Optional) Click Change to find and select an image for the team avatar.
  5. (Optional) Complete the Description field.
  6. Click the Users tab, and select the individuals for the team.

  7. (Optional) Click the Projects tab, and select any uploaded Flare projects to be associated with the team.

  8. Click Save.
