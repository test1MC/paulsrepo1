Editing Teams

How to edit a Team
  1. On the left side of the interface, click Teams.
  2. In the grid, click the name of the team.
  3. On the left side of the dialog, click Edit.
  4. You can use the Settings tab, to change the team name, avatar, and/or description.
  5. You can click the Users tab and select the individuals for the team.

  6. You can click the Projects tab and select any uploaded Flare projects to be associated with the team.

  7. Click Save.
