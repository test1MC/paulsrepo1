About Users

Introduction

Users can work independently, or they can work collaboratively, sending messages to one another, assigning tasks, etc. If you have many users, you can organize them into teams (see About Teams).

Some users might even be added to more than one license. For example, you might be an independent contractor who does work for a couple of companies, each of which has a MadCap Central license. When you log in to Central, you can switch from one company to the other simply by choosing the appropriate license at the top of the interface.

User Types

User Types

There are two user types in Central—Authors and Subject Matter Experts (SMEs). Authors might use any of the features available throughout Central, but SMEs are limited to the file review process. See About Reviews.
