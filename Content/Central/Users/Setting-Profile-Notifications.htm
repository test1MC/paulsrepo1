Which Permission is Required for This Activity?

No special permission is required to edit your profile and notification settings.

 How to Set Your Profile and Notifications
  1. At the top of Central, click your avatar or name, and select Settings.

  2. On the User tab, you can set any of the following in the Profile section.

    Profile Options

    Field

    Description

    [Avatar]

    Click Change if you want to choose an image or reset your avatar. In the dialog that opens, you can click Browse to find and select a new image (2 MB limit). If you click Delete, Central will use your initials for the avatar.

    First Name

    Enter your first name. This field is required.

    Last Name

    Enter your last name. This field is required.

    Initials

    You can enter your initials, which will be used if you have not selected an image for your avatar.

    Title

    You can enter your job title.

    Phone

    You can provide a phone number.

    Cell Phone

    You can provide your cell phone number.

    Location

    You can enter a location, which might be useful if your company has several.

    Department

    You can enter the department or organization where you work in your company.

    Secondary Email

    You can provide a secondary email. This might prove useful if co-workers need an additional way to contact you when you are out of the office.
  3. On the left side of the tab, click Email. You can use the fields in this section to change your email address.

  4. On the left side of the tab, click Password. You can use the fields in this section to change your password.

    Note

    If you belong to more than one MadCap Central license, your password will be changed for all licenses.

  5. On the left side of the tab, click Notifications. Then choose any of the following in order to receive notifications for certain events:

    Send Notifications By

    Field

    Description

    Notification Center

    Select this option if you want to see recent notifications (accessed by clicking the bell icon at the top of the interface). After you open the Notification Center, the alert (red dot) will be removed from the icon.

    Email

    Select this if you want to receive notifications via email. Notifications will be sent to both your primary and secondary email addresses (if the secondary email is provided).
  6. Click Save.
 Notes
