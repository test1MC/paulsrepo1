Assigning Users to Teams and Projects

When creating a team, you can associate both projects and users with it at the same time. So the following steps are really intended for additional users that you want to associate with existing teams and projects.

How to Assign Users to Teams and Projects
  1. Make sure you have already created the team(s) and/or uploaded the Flare project(s). See Creating Teams and Uploading Projects. For more information, see the online Help.
  2. On the left side of the interface, click Users.
  3. Click the user’s name.
  4. On the left, click Assign Teams & Projects.
  5. You can use the Teams tab to select teams, making the user a member of them.
  6. You can click the Projects tab and select any uploaded Flare projects, associating the user with them.
  7. Click Save.
