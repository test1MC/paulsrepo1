Editor Tools

Lingo provides many tools to assist you when you are working in its editors.

  • Backups You can manually create temporary backups of your files. In addition, sometimes creates automatic backups of your files as you work on them. See About Backups.
  • Characters and Symbols You can insert characters and symbols in content files. You can also create quick characters, as well as work with font types and smart quotes. See Inserting Characters and Symbols.
  • Dictionaries You can see which dictionaries are installed on your computer by opening the Options dialog and selecting the Dictionaries tab. You can also select the location where you want to save your global dictionary. See Options Dialog.
  • Find and Replace Lingo provides multiple features for finding and replacing content in your project. See About Find and Replace.
  • Shortcuts lets you work more quickly and efficiently by making use of keyboard shortcuts in a variety of ways. See About Shortcuts.
  • Spell Check You can use spell checking features, based on your built-in and custom dictionaries. See About Spell Check.
  • Thesauruses You can view and select terms related to words in an open translation file. See About the Thesaurus.
