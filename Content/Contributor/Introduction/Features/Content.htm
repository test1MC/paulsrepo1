Content

Primary Content Features

Following are the main types of features used by most Contributor reviewers and authors:

  • Condition Tags
  • Images
  • Links
  • Multimedia
  • Page Layouts
  • Snippets
  • Tables
  • Variables
Additional Content Features

Following are additional types of available features, though not as commonly used by most Contributor reviewers and authors:

  • Concepts
  • Equations
  • Footnotes and Endnotes
  • Indexes
  • QR Codes
  • Slideshows
  • Text Boxes
