Deleting Master Frames

Use the following steps to delete a master frame from the available master frames.

how to Delete a master frame

  1. In the Resources section, select Master Frames.

    The Master Frames window pane opens.

  2. In the Master Frames window pane, select the master frame you want to delete.

    • Local Toolbar Click .
    • Right-Click Select Remove Frame.
    • Keyboard Shortcut Press the DELETE key.
