Logging In to a Video Service

Before using the Video Services window pane, you will need to log in to either YouTube or Vimeo.

How to Log In to a Video Service

Although you are logging in through Mimic, you will still need an account on either Google or Vimeo, depending on the video service type you are using. You will be prompted to log in to your video service account before uploading movies.

How to Log In to YouTube How to Log In to Vimeo Notes

To log out of a video service, click the Logout button in the Video Services window pane.

 What's Next?

After logging on to a video service, you can upload movies. See Uploading Movies to YouTube or Vimeo.
