Adding Comments

All registered users can add comments to the posts (i.e., status updates, questions, articles, and tasks) that people add in Pulse. You can add comments to posts that appear in any topic, group, tag, and user feed that you have access permission to view.

how to add comments
  1. See Logging In to Pulse.
  2. Locate the post where you want to add a comment. You can add comments to status updates, questions, articles, and tasks.
  3. Click the Comment link. Then type your comment in the space provided.

  4. Click the Comment button or press Enter to post your response.
