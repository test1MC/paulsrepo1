Editing Frames

Following are some of the main tasks involved in editing frames.

Frame Editing Tasks

  • Adding You can add new frames to your page layouts in your project, and configure them. See Adding Page Layout Frames.
  • Basic Frames Tasks You can align, remove, rotate, and perform other primary tasks on the frames in your page layouts. See Basic Frames Tasks.
  • Additional Frames Tasks You can insert breaks, text, set anchors, and variables inside your frames. See Additional Frames Tasks.
