Excluding Non-Words from Search

how to exclude non-words from search
  1. In the Project Organizer, open the desired target.
  2. In the Target Editor, click the Search tab.
  3. In the Search Results section, click the drop-down arrow next to Advanced Search Options.
  4. Place a check mark in the Exclude non-words from search box. To include non-words in search, clear the check mark.
