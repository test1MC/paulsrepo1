Customizing Writing Suggestions Shown

If you want to limit or expand the list of the writing suggestions that are displayed, you can easily do so by selecting various options.

how to customize the writing suggestions shown in the window pane
  1. View the writing suggestions by selecting the Analysis ribbon. Then click Suggestions > Writing Suggestions.
  2. In the local toolbar of the Writing Suggestions window pane, click Search Properties. The Writing Suggestions Search Properties dialog opens.

  3. Select any of the options in the dialog.
