Enabling and Disabling Partial-Word Search for Indexes

By default, partial-word searching is enabled for HTML5 Tripane indexes in the output. See About Indexes.

how to Enable or Disable Partial-Word Search for Indexes
  1. Open an Tripane skin.

  2. On the General tab of the Skin Editor, under Index Options, select Partial Word Search. Removing the check mark disables the feature.

 What's Next?

Make sure that you associate the skin with the target. See Associating Skins with Targets.
